Why SEO is Important for Beginners?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving a website to increase its visibility on search engines like Google. Beginners can learn SEO by creating simple websites using HTML and practicing on-page SEO techniques. This includes writing good titles, adding meta descriptions, using headings, and optimizing images with alt text. It’s also important to use relevant keywords and create internal links between your pages.

SEO Learning Goals

  • Use proper SEO tags like title, meta, headings
  • Practice step-by-step on-page SEO implementation

Common SEO Mistakes to Avoid

Many beginners forget to use alt text on images, heading tags, and meta descriptions. Always structure your content clearly and provide useful internal links to other pages like about SEO or get support.
